About

Have you ever wanted to learn how to hack or program a computer? How about learning how to create websites or make games? Do you want to test your skills in programming competitions and meet other developers? If you said yes to any of these questions, then the CS Club is the club for you!

The Computer Science Club offers opportunities to learn coding essentials and collaborate in programming projects. We offer a fun environment to participate in computer programming and development with no programming experience required. Even if you already have a good understanding of how to program, the CS Club allows you to meet and work with other developers as well as compete in programming and hacking challenges. No matter what your interests or experiences are, we hope to see you there!
